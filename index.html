Resume

Flone is an Android app and airframe that creates a flying platform for smartphones. The airframe is a quadcopter designed for carry a smartphone, battery, and a good field of view for the two cameras of the smartphone. The Flone Remote app allows the communication between the smartphone of the pilot and the Multiwii flightboard (over bluetooth).

Flone Remote

  • Read orientation and inclination of the phone.
  • Read position of finger in screen.
  • Transform reads to rc values, embed in Multiwii Serial Protocol and sendit.
  • Visual/Audio Feedback.
